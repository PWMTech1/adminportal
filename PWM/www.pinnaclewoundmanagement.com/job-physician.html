We're Hiring

Wound Care Phyician

place Charlottesville, VA

Pinnacle Wound Management is currently seeking a wound specialist to join our network. We welcome surgeons and physicians with all specialty backgrounds. Our partnered specialist visits the patient at the nursing home and develops a comprehensive plan of care with the nursing staff.

Pinnacle Wound Management is one of the nation’s fastest-growing network of highly trained wound care providers. Our wound specialists enjoy a work-life balance. Our specialist round weekly from 7am-4pm. No weekends, No nights, No calls.

Interested in joining the Pinnacle Wound Management family?

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Travel to each assigned nursing facility weekly
  • Assess patient and make recommendations on plan of care
  • Work closely with nursing staff and facility administration
  • Utilize facility formulary and follow facility protocols
  • Document patient encounter in our EHR to ensure timely reporting
  • Discuss treatments and goals with PCP and family members
  • Procedures – Debridement’s, I & D’s, Cauterization, NPWT, etc.

Benefits:

  • No Weekends
  • No On-call
  • No Nights
  • Part Time or Full Time
  • Competitive Compensation
  • Schedule tailored to your availability
  • Continuing Education
  • Full malpractice coverage

Requirements:

  • Unrestricted Medical License in state of practice
  • DEA
  • Must pass state and federal background checks
  • Basic EHR knowledge
  • Team Player
  • Strong Communication skills
  • 1 year wound care experience
  • Wound Care Certification (preferred)
