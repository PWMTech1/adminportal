Our Services

The Pinnacle Wound Management service model emphasizes value-delivery in wound care. Our affiliated providers treat our patients in person and via telemedicine regardless of their insurance or ability to pay.

Who We Serve

Our goal is to improve the standards of wound care and the lives of the patients we treat. We provide consultation and care services to every patient we encounter regardless of their payor source. Our nursing team and affiliated physicians treat our patients at the following locations.

  • Nurse Facility
  • Assisted Living Facility
  • Independent Living Facility
  • LTAC

Wound Debridement

Pinnacle Wound Management offers highly skilled specialists with years of experience in wound debridement procedures.

The removal of devitalized tissue, via debridement, is the cornerstone of wound management.

Nail Debridements

Nail debridement involves the removal of a diseased toenail bed or viable nail plate.

Nursing Assistance & Education

We seek to grow your bedside staff during all visits by our affiliated specialist to obtain the best wound prevention and treatment solutions. We provide a broad portfolio of education programs to our facility clients because we recognize the importance of having a philosophically aligned team.

Tube Replacement

Pinnacle Wound Management has a team of highly skilled professionals that perform PEG tube replacements, suprapubic tube replacements, and tracheostomy tube replacements. We offer routine changes following the manufactures recommendations.

Standarized Care

Cutting-edge technology is frequently underutilized and neglected in long-term care for wound patients. We are attempting to dismantle these barriers. Wound assessment and care is a rapidly evolving science. As a result, we take satisfaction in introducing several cutting-edge technologies to the long-term care setting.

What’s more, we regularly collaborate with wound care suppliers to keep our providers updated and informed on the appropriate utilization of the equipment.

Cost-Effective Treatment Plan

At Pinnacle Wound Management, we put you in charge. Our providers utilize products from the nursing home’s contracted wound care supplier. However, we ensure that we recommend the appropriate products that follow the best practice of care.

Online Facility Portal

Worried about misplacing a specific visit note? Our facility portal will provide you instant access to recommendations and patient visit history. You can visit our facility portal to retrieve all patient visit records, quality measures reporting, utilization, and various other reports.

Analysis Reporting

Our comprehensive platform is meant to offer enhanced wound analytics to our facility partners. Real-time analysis of wound data, such as the percentage of participants consulted, the number of wounds, healing rates, and other data points, is part of the analytics. This knowledge allows facility managers to see trends and take appropriate action. Our clients have access to these analytics at no cost.

Compliance

We have an integrated HIPAA-compliant platform that is in line with MDS guidelines and contains all necessary documentation required at the state and federal levels.

Why Choose Us?

Pinnacle Wound Management has established a benchmark in surpassing clients' expectations, based on solid concepts of client care and ethical behavior. Our infrastructure, combined with our integrated effort, allows clients to take full use of our team's size and expertise while keeping costs low. Our clients have access to a wide range of services beyond state lines thanks to the integration of industry knowledge and geographical regions through innovative technology.

Technology

Our EHR was developed with wound care in mind. This approach is meant to ensure that our collaborating facilities receive the most full and detailed status reports as quickly as possible. Regardless of internet availability, our web-based solution enables real-time recordkeeping.

Our real-time wound specialist documentation eliminates the need for notes to be transcribed and avoids unnecessary delays. During wound visits, the wound team documents the visit, providing precise and error-free records that are shared with the health care team during the visit.

Interoperability

Our technology integrates directly with a variety of facility EHR systems. Our system can retrieve patient information and send our visit notice to the patient's electronic chart directly.

Your nurses will have access to all the consultation notes straight on their platform. They won't have to rely on email or fax to get information.

We save precious nursing hours gathering face sheets, vitals, medical background, diagnosis, and even building a patient list by connecting with your EHR system.
